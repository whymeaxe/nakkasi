The Art of Nakkashi

In the heart of Udaipur, our master artisans practice Nakkashi—the ancient art of ornate wood carving. Every curve, every inlay, every grain is treated with reverence.

Using premium woods and time-honored techniques passed through generations, we create furniture that transcends mere functionality. These are pieces designed to be felt, admired, and treasured.

✓
Hand-selected premium woods from sustainable sources
✓
