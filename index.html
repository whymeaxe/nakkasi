Crafted in the City of Lakes

Nakkasi was born from a deep reverence for Udaipur's rich artisanal heritage. Our story began in 1947 when our founder, Master Craftsman Vidhata Sharma, established a small workshop dedicated to preserving the ancient art of Nakkashi—the intricate wood carving technique that has defined our city for centuries.

Today, we are proud to be one of Udaipur's premier luxury furniture makers, trusted by discerning clients across India and beyond. Our workshop remains in the same historic district, where third-generation artisans continue to hand-craft each piece with the same passion and precision.

Every item that leaves our atelier carries with it a piece of Udaipur's soul—a testament to the skill, patience, and artistry that has been passed down through generations of master craftsmen.

Product Overview

The Maharaja Luxury Sofa represents the finest expression of traditional Indian furniture making. Crafted from hand-selected aged teak, each piece undergoes months of meticulous work by our master artisans.

The frame features traditional mortise and tenon joinery, ensuring structural integrity that will last for generations. Every curve and detail is hand-carved using the ancient Nakkashi technique, creating intricate patterns that tell a story of heritage and artistry.
